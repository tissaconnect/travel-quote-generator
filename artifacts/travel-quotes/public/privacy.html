Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

Travolo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use travoloapp.com and our quote generation service.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Your name, email address, and agency details when you create an account
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe — we never store your card details
  • Quote content you paste into the app to generate client presentations
  • Usage data such as number of quotes generated and features used

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Travolo service
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send you service-related communications and support responses
  • Understand how advisors use the product to improve features
  • Comply with legal obligations

Quote Content

The raw quote text you paste into Travolo is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to extract and format hotel and pricing information. This content is processed to generate your client one-pager and is not stored permanently on our servers beyond what is needed to complete that request. Please refer to Anthropic's privacy policy for details on how they handle API data.

Important: Do not paste personally identifiable client information such as passport numbers, dates of birth, or financial details into the quote generator. Travolo is designed for hotel and pricing quote content only.

Shareable Links

When you generate a shareable client link, the HTML content of that quote is stored on our servers with a unique ID so your client can view it. These links are not indexed or publicly searchable. You can request deletion of any stored quote by contacting us.

Third Party Services

We use the following third party services:

  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management
  • Clerk — user authentication and account management
  • Anthropic — AI processing of quote content
  • Replit — application hosting and infrastructure

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. Your password is never stored — we use passwordless authentication via email verification codes. Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and never touches our servers.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription and request account deletion we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights email us at support@travoloapp.com and we will respond within 5 business days.

Children's Privacy

Travolo is intended for use by travel professionals and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on the app. Continued use of Travolo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
